### Timing

## Consolidated intent
Two related display experiences for the "Wet Greg" character:
1. **Boot splash** (~10s) — quick, sassy entrance with title reveal, then hands off to the existing quotes screen.
2. **Introduction/tutorial** — longer emotive narrative that introduces Greg (a lone, wet octopus far from home) with animated mood cues and slowly-revealed story text.

Both experiences must support **landscape** and **portrait** orientations, with entrance direction and title placement adapted per orientation.
